Bellabeat is a high-tech manufacturer of health-focused products for women. Their products include wearable smart jewelry, smart bottles, health tracking apps, and membership programs for personal guidance. The co-founders of the company believe that analyzing smart device fitness data could help unlock new growth opportunities for the company so that they become more successful in the global smart device market.

Prepare

Data Source

FitBit Fitness Tracker Data is a public domain dataset made available through Mobius. The data was collected by Amazon Mechanical Turk between March 12, 2016 and May 12, 2016, and last updated on December 16, 2020. This Kaggle dataset consists of 18 CSV files and contains personal fitness tracker from thirty FitBit users. Thirty eligible Fitbit users consented to the submission of personal tracker data, including minute-level output for physical activity, heart rate, and sleep monitoring. It includes information about daily activity, steps, and heart rate that can be used to explore usersâ€™ habits.

Data Credibility

A good data source is Reliable, Original, Comprehensive, Current, and Cited (ROCCC). We can analyze the credibility of our dataset based on ROCCC aspects.

  1. Reliable: LOW (Small sample size with 30 individual participants.)

  2. Original: LOW (Third-party information.)

  3. Comprehensive: MEDIUM (Parameters are mostly related to Bellabeatâ€™s products but they may contain human error.)

  4. Current: LOW (Data is 8 years old.)

  5. Cited: LOW (Data was collected and made public by third-party providers.)

Act

In the last phase, we will clarify the trends in smart device usage based on the data we analyzed, and make recommendations so that we can turn our insights into actions.

Trends

  1. There is a positive correlation between total steps taken and calories burnt in a day.

  2. There is a positive correlation between total time spent very actively and calories burnt in a day.

  3. 81.33% of the users spend their time sedentary during a day.

  4. Users sleep approximately 7 hours a day.

Recommendations

  1. A summary of total steps taken per day can be shared with the users weekly. They can set their own goals on how many steps they want to take per day, and get an encouraging notification if they fail. There can also be a streak tracker for how many consecutive days the user achieves the daily goal to keep the user motivated.

  2. It should be encouraged that users spend more very active time even if it is short, rather than long but lightly active or sedentary time. The live data can be shared with the users so that they can set their goals on how much very active time they want to spend.

  3. Users should be notified after a certain time of inactivity in order to shorten their sedentary time.

  4. Professionals recommend adults get 7-9 hours of sleep a day. Users should be warned on days when they sleep less or more than this range and the importance of sleep for health should be emphasized. A streak tracker for consecutive days with appropriate amount of sleep can be used in order to motivate the users.

  5. All these suggestions can be integrated into a membership plan and presented to users, enabling the company to create an additional source of income. By offering a free trial period, users can become more familiar with the features. In this way, we can reach more users, collect more data, and improve the features for our own users by analyzing our own data.
